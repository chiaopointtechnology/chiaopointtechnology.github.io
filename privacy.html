隱私政策Privacy Policy

最後更新：2026 年 3 月Last updated: March 2026

喬點科技有限公司（以下簡稱「我們」）重視您的隱私。本隱私政策適用於我們開發與營運的所有應用程式，包括但不限於 Petit a Petit、ClosetTime 衣櫃時光、FoodMODAPP 及未來推出的產品。CHIAOPOINT Technology Co., Ltd. ("we", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all apps we develop and operate, including but not limited to Petit a Petit, ClosetTime, FoodMODAPP, and future products.

1. 我們收集哪些資料1. What We Collect

我們可能根據您使用的 App 功能收集以下資料：We may collect the following data depending on the app features you use:

  • 位置資料 — 用於跑步追蹤（Petit a Petit）、天氣查詢（ClosetTime）。僅在您授權時使用。Location Data — For run tracking (Petit a Petit) and weather queries (ClosetTime). Used only when you authorize.
  • 照片 — 用於衣物照片儲存（ClosetTime）。僅儲存於您的裝置或 iCloud，不會上傳至我們的伺服器。Photos — For clothing photo storage (ClosetTime). Stored only on your device or iCloud, never uploaded to our servers.
  • 帳號資訊 — Email 地址（透過 Apple 或 Google 登入）與匿名用戶 ID。Account Info — Email address (via Apple or Google sign-in) and anonymous user ID.
  • 購買紀錄 — App 內購買項目，僅用於功能解鎖驗證。Purchase Records — In-app purchase items, used only for feature unlock verification.
  • 使用數據 — App 互動行為、功能使用頻率（匿名化處理）。Usage Data — App interactions and feature usage frequency (anonymized).
  • 健身數據 — 跑步距離、時間、配速（Petit a Petit）。Fitness Data — Running distance, time, and pace (Petit a Petit).

2. 資料使用方式2. How We Use Data

  • 提供並維護 App 核心功能（追蹤、推薦、同步）Provide and maintain core app features (tracking, recommendations, sync)
  • 改善用戶體驗與修復錯誤Improve user experience and fix bugs
  • 分析使用趨勢以優化產品（匿名化數據）Analyze usage trends for product optimization (anonymized data)

我們不會出售您的個人資料給任何第三方。
我們不會將您的資料用於廣告投放。We do NOT sell your personal data to any third party.
We do NOT use your data for advertising.

3. 資料儲存與安全3. Data Storage & Security

  • 本地儲存 — 資料儲存於您的裝置（SwiftData / CoreData）Local Storage — Data stored on your device (SwiftData / CoreData)
  • 雲端同步 — 透過 Apple CloudKit 或 Firebase Firestore 加密傳輸同步Cloud Sync — Synced via Apple CloudKit or Firebase Firestore with encrypted transmission
  • 照片儲存 — 僅存於您的裝置或 iCloud，不會上傳至我們的伺服器Photo Storage — Stored only on your device or iCloud, never on our servers

4. 第三方服務4. Third-Party Services

我們的 App 可能使用以下第三方服務，這些服務有各自的隱私政策：Our apps may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Apple CloudKit — iCloud 資料同步Apple CloudKit — iCloud data sync
  • Firebase — 雲端資料庫、分析、錯誤報告Firebase — Cloud database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Open-Meteo — 天氣 API（不收集個人資料）Open-Meteo — Weather API (no personal data collected)
  • Apple App Store — 應用內購買處理Apple App Store — In-app purchase processing

5. 您的權利5. Your Rights

  • 隨時刪除您的帳號與所有相關資料（在 App 設定中操作）Delete your account and all related data anytime (in app settings)
  • 停用雲端同步功能Disable cloud sync features
  • 撤銷位置、照片或其他權限Revoke location, photo, or other permissions
  • 聯絡我們查詢或刪除您的個人資料Contact us to inquire about or delete your personal data

6. 兒童隱私6. Children's Privacy

我們的服務不針對 13 歲以下的兒童。我們不會故意收集 13 歲以下兒童的個人資料。如果您是家長並發現您的孩子提供了個人資料，請聯絡我們，我們將立即刪除。Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. 政策變更7. Policy Changes

我們可能會不定期更新本隱私政策。變更後的政策將在本頁面公佈，並更新「最後更新」日期。We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
