CHIAOPOINT TECHNOLOGY LLC ("we", "us") values your privacy. 1. What We Collect

For Mobile App Users:

  • Location Data — For GPS-based features such as route tracking. Collected only when you authorize.
  • Account Info — Email address (via Apple or Google sign-in) and anonymous user ID.
  • Purchase Records — In-app purchase items, used only for feature unlock verification.

For AI Business Solution Clients:

  • Business Information — Business name, location, contact details, and industry type.
  • Operational Data — Inventory levels, sales history, and menu information for AI recommendations.
  • Communication Identifiers — LINE User ID, phone number, or email for service notifications.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide and maintain core product features (tracking, sync, AI predictions, notifications)
  • Generate AI-powered recommendations and insights for business clients
  • Improve user experience and fix bugs

We do NOT sell your personal data to any third party.

We do NOT use your data for advertising.

We do NOT share business data between different clients.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Mobile Apps — Data stored locally on your device and synced via Apple CloudKit or Firebase with encrypted transmission
  • AI Solutions — Business data stored in secured cloud services, accessible only to the respective client and our system
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption

4. Third-Party Services

Our products may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase — Cloud database, authentication, analytics, crash reporting
  • Apple CloudKit — iCloud data sync
  • Apple App Store — In-app purchase processing
  • OpenAI API — AI prediction and analysis engine
  • LINE Messaging API — Service notifications (Asia-Pacific)
  • Google Services — Cloud data management for AI solutions
  • Open-Meteo — Weather API (no personal data collected)

5. Your Rights

  • Delete your account and all related data anytime (in app settings or by contacting us)
  • Disable cloud sync or notification features
  • Revoke location, photo, or other permissions
  • Request a copy of your personal data in a standard format
  • Contact us to inquire about or delete your personal data

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.
