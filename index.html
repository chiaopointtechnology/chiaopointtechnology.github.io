Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

CHIAOPOINT TECHNOLOGY LLC ("we", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all apps we develop and operate.

1. What We Collect

We may collect the following data depending on the app features you use:

  • Location Data — For run tracking and weather queries. Used only when you authorize.
  • Photos — For clothing photo storage. Stored only on your device or iCloud, never uploaded to our servers.
  • Account Info — Email address (via Apple or Google sign-in) and anonymous user ID.
  • Purchase Records — In-app purchase items, used only for feature unlock verification.
  • Usage Data — App interactions and feature usage frequency (anonymized).
  • Fitness Data — Running distance, time, and pace.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide and maintain core app features (tracking, recommendations, sync)
  • Improve user experience and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage trends for product optimization (anonymized data)

We do NOT sell your personal data to any third party.

We do NOT use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage — Data stored on your device (SwiftData / CoreData)
  • Cloud Sync — Synced via Apple CloudKit or Firebase Firestore with encrypted transmission
  • Photo Storage — Stored only on your device or iCloud, never on our servers

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Apple CloudKit — iCloud data sync
  • Firebase — Cloud database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Open-Meteo — Weather API (no personal data collected)
  • Apple App Store — In-app purchase processing

5. Your Rights

  • Delete your account and all related data anytime (in app settings)
  • Disable cloud sync features
  • Revoke location, photo, or other permissions
  • Contact us to inquire about or delete your personal data

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
