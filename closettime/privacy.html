Privacy Policy

ClosetTime / 衣櫃時光

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Overview

ClosetTime ("we", "our", "the App") is a smart closet management app developed by CHINJUNG WENG. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Photos

  • Clothing photos you take or select from your photo library
  • Stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud (if enabled)
  • We do NOT upload your photos to any external server

2.2 Location Data

  • Used solely to fetch local weather information for outfit recommendations
  • Location data is sent to Open-Meteo (weather API) to retrieve weather conditions
  • We do NOT store or track your location history

2.3 Clothing Data

  • Item names, categories, colors, brands, prices, purchase dates, and notes you enter
  • Wear records, outfit combinations, and daily diary entries
  • Stored locally on your device and synced via Apple iCloud/CloudKit

2.4 Purchase Data

  • In-app purchase records are managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit
  • We do NOT have access to your payment information

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Closet Management: Display and organize your clothing items
  • Outfit Recommendations: Use weather data and wear history to suggest outfits
  • Statistics: Calculate wardrobe value, cost-per-wear, style distribution
  • Cloud Sync: Sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud/CloudKit: For data synchronization across your devices. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Open-Meteo: For weather data. We send only latitude/longitude coordinates. Subject to Open-Meteo's Terms.
  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchases. Managed entirely by Apple.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Cloud sync uses Apple's CloudKit with end-to-end encryption
  • We do NOT operate any external servers or databases
  • Photos are compressed and stored in your app's private container

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your data persists as long as the app is installed on your device
  • Deleting the app removes all local data
  • iCloud data can be managed through your device's iCloud settings
  • You can delete individual items at any time within the app

8. Children's Privacy

ClosetTime is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Delete any or all of your data
  • Disable iCloud sync in your device settings
  • Deny camera or location permissions at any time

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
